About Me :

I am an Assistant Professor at University of Warwick. Previously I was a Research Scientist at TCS Innovation Labs, Hyderabad. Prior to this I was a visiting scientist at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata, this is also the place where I wrote my Ph D thesis "Combinatorial Aspects in Construction of Cryptographically Significant Boolean Functions under different Symmetry Conditions" in 2008. I spent two years as a post doctoral fellow at SECRET, INRIA Paris-Rocquencourt followed by being a post doctoral fellow at the Department of Computer Science , University of Calgary.
